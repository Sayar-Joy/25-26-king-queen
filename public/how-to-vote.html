How to vote

Follow these simple steps to cast your vote:

  1. Browse the categories using the Selections menu.
  2. Swipe or click through candidates and review their profiles.
  3. Click the Vote button on the candidate you choose.
  4. Confirm your selection in any confirmation dialog that appears.
  5. Only one vote per category is allowed — make it count.

If you have questions, contact the election committee.
